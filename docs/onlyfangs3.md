# OnlyFangs 3 Requirements Mapping

| # | OF3 Requirement | HC Plus Feature | Phase | Priority |
|---|----------------|-----------------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Fractions within faction split | Fractions system with team aggregation | 6 | Critical |
| 2 | Point system for heroics with difficulty ratings | Scoring engine with community-voted ratings | 5 | Critical |
| 3 | Thousands of micro-achievements | Achievement tracker (100+ at launch, extensible) | 5 | High |
| 4 | Player rating (1–10) for fair drafts | Rating system with draft budget and UI | 6 | High |

## Coverage

- **Critical:** 4/4 (Fractions, Scoring, Death Tracking, Leaderboards)
- **High:** 5/5 (Achievements, Draft, Punishments, GM Tools, Random Events)
- **Medium:** 4/4 (Rewards, Weekly Reports, Hidden Surprises, Stream Data)
- **Low (future):** 1/1 (Non-WoW Events)
