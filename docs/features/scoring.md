# Scoring & Rating System

Two scoring systems proposed. Community votes or GM selects per guild.

---

## Option 1: Complex Scoring (Multiplier-Based)

```
Score = BaseValue * LevelMultiplier * DifficultyMultiplier * SurvivorBonus
```

- **BaseValue:** Fixed per action type (boss kill = 100, dungeon clear = 50, etc.)
- **LevelMultiplier:** Player level relative to content level
- **DifficultyMultiplier:** Community-voted difficulty per dungeon/boss
- **SurvivorBonus:** Scaled by surviving group members

## Option 2: Simple Scoring (Fixed Voted Values)

Community votes on fixed point values per action. Examples:
- Prince Malchezaar kill: +500 per survivor
- Heroic Shattered Halls clear: +300
- Reaching level 70: +1000
- Death at level 60+: -200

---

## Point Sources

### Boss Kills (Scaled by Difficulty)
Points awarded per surviving player. Community-voted difficulty ratings per boss.

### Micro-Achievements
Thousands of possibilities:
- First player to reach fishing 375: +250
- First to clear all Nagrand quests deathless: +500
- Kill 1000 Fel Orcs: +200
- Die to fatigue: -50 (shame points)

## Display

- **In-Game Panel:** Dockable, resizable (like Details! or Deathlog). Shows personal score, guild leaderboard, fraction standings, recent achievements.
- **Website:** Updated every few minutes via companion app. Full leaderboards, historical charts, player profiles.
