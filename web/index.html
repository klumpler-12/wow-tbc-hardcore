All Content Rulesets: PvE & PvP

Play everything. Adjust rules. Still get punished.

TBC content is vast. Play everything with massive incentives, but remember: nothing is without consequences upon failing.

  • PvE Focus (All Modes): Adjust rules to make high-end raids possible, translating permadeath into gear drops or heavy score loss.
  • PvP Focus (Hybrid): Enable special rules for Arenas and BGs where death results in gear lockouts or rank loss instead of deletion.

Anti-Boost Detection

Optional toggle to prevent power-leveling. Detects when a high-level player is carrying a lower-level character.

Scanning for Infractions

The addon precisely monitors your party and raid composition. Any mismatched groups with significant level disparities are immediately flagged. Characters boosted by grouping with high-level players can be automatically voided, providing complete transparency to Guild Masters.
