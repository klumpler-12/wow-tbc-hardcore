Hardcore Plus

The Ultimate TBC Challenge. Stand out as a verified elite player by conquering Outland with the highest stakes.

Like Retro, but adapted for TBC. Strict rules with critical adjustments to make Outland progression feasible.

⚰️Forgiving Permadeath — 1 death allowed before deletion
🔓Checkpoint boost: reach 70 once → unlock 58 boost for alts
📊Account-wide scoring — quests, dungeons, achievements
🏷️Auto-generated tags expose your rules at a glance

Hybrid Hardcore

Your Guild, Your Rules. Enable dynamic events where deaths mean punishment, not the immediate end of the journey.

Built for streamers and custom events. Deeply customizable rules mixing hardcore tension with TBC needs.

⚙️Guild Master rules: Multiple lives before reaching permadeath
⚔️Penalties over deletion: Gear deletion or massive score drops
🎁Save your progress: Checkpoints upon completing specific zones
Severity

All Content Rulesets: PvE & PvP

Play everything. Adjust rules. Still get punished.

TBC content is vast. Play everything with massive incentives, but remember: nothing is without consequences upon failing.

  • PvE Focus (All Modes): Adjust rules to make high-end raids possible, translating permadeath into gear drops or heavy score loss.
  • PvP Focus (Hybrid): Enable special rules for Arenas and BGs where death results in gear lockouts or rank loss instead of deletion.
